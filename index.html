About us

Welcome to Complete Blasting Solutions

Founded in 2019, Complete Blasting Solutions has been at the forefront of the sandblasting and waterblasting industry, offering comprehensive and advanced cleaning solutions. Our goal is to provide exceptional service while adhering to the highest standards of environmental responsibility.

  • Our expertise lies in delivering superior waterblasting services for a variety of needs - be it residential, commercial, or industrial. We pride ourselves on using the latest technology to ensure efficient and effective cleaning without causing harm to surfaces.
  • We are committed to eco-friendly practices. Our techniques are designed to reduce water usage and avoid harsh chemicals, making our services safe for both our clients and the environment..
  • Customer satisfaction is our priority. With our skilled team, state-of-the-art equipment, and dedication to excellence, we guarantee a service that meets your highest expectations..

Services

Check our Services

Cleaning

Tailored to each project, our services ensure thorough, high-quality cleaning for both commercial and residential needs. We use high-pressure waterblasting to remove dirt, grime, and other contaminants from surfaces, leaving them clean and looking brand new.

Grafity Removal

We specialize in swift and effective graffiti removal. Utilizing advanced waterblasting technology, we safely erase unwanted graffiti from a variety of surfaces, including walls, sidewalks, and public installations. Our approach not only removes graffiti but also protects the underlying surface from damage.

Restoration Work

We excel in restoration work, leveraging our advanced waterblasting techniques to revive and rejuvenate surfaces. Our process is ideal for restoring historical buildings, aged infrastructures, and weathered exteriors. We carefully remove layers of dirt, grime, and paint, revealing the original beauty of the structure without causing harm.

Automotive Restoration

We specialize in restoring classic and vintage vehicles, carefully removing years of accumulated dirt, rust, and old paint. Our gentle yet effective approach ensures that every detail of the vehicle is treated with respect, preserving its original charm and character.

Wood Refinishing

Our skilled professionals use gentle waterblasting techniques combined with fine craftsmanship to strip away old paint, stains, and varnishes. We meticulously restore the natural beauty and texture of the wood, preparing it for staining or sealing.

Concrete Profiling

our concrete profiling service is designed to expertly prepare concrete surfaces for coatings, sealants, or overlays. Utilizing advanced waterblasting techniques, we efficiently create the ideal surface texture, ensuring optimal adhesion for subsequent treatments. This process removes contaminants, levels uneven areas, and etches the surface, making it perfectly primed for further refinishing.

Get in touch with us today to discuss your needs and discover how our expert services can make a difference. Your satisfaction is our promise!
